About Redline

Something about company
We are a Trusted Name in the Auto Industry
Serving the Greater Omaha Area!

Red Line Auto is a local licensed car dealership in the Greater Omaha Area with over 5 years of experience serving our local customers with their needs. We are located in the City of Omaha off of I-80 on 84th Street.

Our inventory is acquired directly from major insurance companies and through trade-ins which allows us to pass the savings over to our local and nationwide customers. We are here to help customers with their every need when it comes to buying a car. Please contact us directly for any questions and concerns or stop by our lot to test drive and inspect the vehicle in person. If you would like, you can bring your mechanic to inspect the vehicle you are interested in. We highly recommend that you have the car inspected by an independent mechanic.

  • Affordable Auto Prices Redline has very competitive prices.
  • 5 Years in Business Redline has been in the business of repairing and selling automobiles since 2014 and hope to continue serving the area for years to come.
  • Trusted by Auto Buyers When it comes to buying a car, Red Line is here to help.
